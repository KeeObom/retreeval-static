Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your common questions about Retreeval’s features, pricing, security, and more.

What types of documents can I upload and chat with?

You can upload PDFs, Word documents (.doc and .docx), Excel spreadsheets (.xlsx), CSV files, and plain text files (.txt). Our AI indexes the content so you can ask questions naturally.

Which databases does Retreeval support for natural language querying?

Retreeval supports popular SQL databases including PostgreSQL, MySQL, ClickHouse, Snowflake, BigQuery, and others. We continually expand support for new data sources.

How secure is my data with Retreeval?

Security is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure to protect your documents and queries. Your data is never shared without your consent.

Can I collaborate with my team on insights and chats?

Absolutely! Retreeval allows you to share chat sessions, documents, and query results securely across your organization to foster teamwork and faster decision-making.

What are the pricing plans and limits on usage?

We offer tiered pricing plans including Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise, with varying storage, user limits, and database connections. Visit our Pricing page for detailed information.

How do I get started with Retreeval?

Simply sign up for a free account, upload your documents or connect your databases, and start asking questions right away with our intuitive AI-powered interface.
